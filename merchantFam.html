˜Dale and Patricia Merchant˜

Patricia was born 1938 to Thomas and Muriel "Grace" Price in Castor, Alberta. Dale and Patriica married December 1960, throughout their life they lived in different places Alberta, Canada while raising Dale "Scott", Brad, Christopher "Chris", and their adoptive daughter Jill. Patricia worked at the Lethbridge Community College in the Transitional Training Program until her illness of ALS prevented her from doing so and passed October 1993. Dale was born 1937 in Coronation, Alberta to Robert and Jessie Merchant. Jessie Merchant had the last name changed from Merchand to Merchant, she said Merchand sounded too French. Dale worked as a social worker until retirement and was a very active member is the Rotary Club. Dale now has 9 grandchildren and 8 great-grandchildren. Jill is now an EMT with 4 children(Kennedy, Jeff, Jordan, Carling) and 5 grandchildren, Chris is now a PA with 2 children (Corbin and Jordyn), Brad has 3 children(Hayley, Sara, and Scotty) and 3 grandchildren. He just recently moved back to Canada from Dubai as the mall manager and is deciding if he wants to continue with that, and Scott is enjoying life day by day being his strange self.

˜Chris and Laura Merchant˜

Chris went to the University of Montana to become a physical therapist where he met his wife Laura. Their first few years of marriage they were floating around Montana but eventually landed in Power, Montana where they had their daughter, Jordyn, July 1996. Shortly after that Chris went back to school to become a PA which is when they decided it would be best to move into Great Falls and then had their son, Corbin, December 1998. Laura is now the CEO of the Chester Hospital, but is planning to accept a job at Benefis soon, and Chris has been working for the same orthopedic surgeons the last 23 years as thier PA. Jordyn had a hard time deciding at first on what she wanted to do but is now on her third year working full time as a medical coder at the hospital and going to the local college as a full time student for computer programming and cybersecurity. Corbin graduated from MSU Bozeman last winter and has now started his first year of medical school through the WAMI Program in Bozeman where he hopes to become a surgeon but isn't sure of what specialty yet.

˜Abbie Price Harrison˜

Abbie Price Harrison was born in Dublin Ireland 1825. She married John Harrison in Toronto, Canada but they eventually moved their large family to Minneapolis where both John and Abbie are burried. It was down Johns' family line that I discovered my ancestors are part of the origional 13 colonies.

˜LaFortune & Lamont˜

Asarie Darie Marie Tellier dit LaFortune was born October 1807 in Quebec, Canada to Louis and Marie. She married Joseph Archambault who she had 6 children with. Joseph died and then she married Jean Baptiste Muloin who she had 9 children with. Janet Brown-Laurie "Jennet" Lamont was born 1827 in Lanarkshite Scotland. She came over to Canada with her family where she would marry John Henry Walker Brown in Ontario, Canada 1846 who she had 7 children with he later died after they had been married for 23 years, she would remarry then later pass in Ontario, Canada at the age of 78.

˜Whiteside & MacGillivray˜

Frank Whiteside was a military man who was born 1873 in Ontario Canada to Henry and Hannah Whiteside. He first married Daisy Bell McGillivray Whiteside, daughter of Jessie Laurie Brown McGillivray, who he had 8 children with. After Daisy passed from childbirth complications Frank married her sister Christina McKay Margaret MacGillvray and had another 8 children together. Frank also had a son from another relationship.Frank died September 29, 1916 in Coronation, Alberta after a man shot him on the courthouse steps for having an affair with his wife.
